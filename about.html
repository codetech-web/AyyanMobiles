Welcome to Ayyan Mobiles, one of the most trusted mobile stores dedicated to providing the latest smartphones, genuine accessories, and excellent customer service.

Established on 04/07/2008, Ayyan Mobiles has proudly served customers for many years with quality products from top brands such as Apple, Samsung, Redmi, and more.

Under the visionary leadership of our Founder - PR MUTHUKUMAR and Co-Founder - RITHIK MUTHUKUMAR, our store has grown into a trusted destination for smartphone lovers.

We focus on delivering 100% original mobiles, affordable prices, exciting offers, and flexible EMI options to make every customer’s shopping experience smooth and reliable.

Since our beginning in 2008, customer satisfaction has always been our first priority. We believe in trust, quality, and long-term relationships with our customers.

Our Mission: To provide customers with the latest smartphones, genuine accessories, affordable prices, and excellent service.

Our Vision: To become the most preferred mobile store by offering the latest technology at the best price.
